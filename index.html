The Science

WBGT — The Formula
Behind Every Alert

Wet Bulb Globe Temperature is the gold standard for measuring heat stress on workers. Karuna calculates it on-chip every 30 seconds.

WBGT = 0.7 × Tw + 0.2 × Tg + 0.1 × Ta
ISO 7933 International Standard — applied to every helmet in real time
Tw — Wet bulb temp (70% weight) · DHT22 humidity conversion
Tg — Globe temp (20% weight) · IR radiant heat estimation
Ta — Air temp (10% weight) · DHT22 direct reading
WBGT thresholds — Karuna response levels
What happens at each danger level, automatically
WBGT Range Risk Level Helmet Response Supervisor Alert
< 28°C Safe Green LED — normal work continues No action needed
28–32°C Caution Amber buzzer — reduce heavy work Optional SMS nudge
32–35°C High Risk Orange alert — mandatory 15-min rest SMS fired instantly
> 35°C Danger Red SOS — evacuate zone NOW Twilio alert + GPS pin
Worker Wellbeing

Policy & Compliance
Framework

Karuna doesn't just protect workers — it keeps employers legally compliant with Indian labour laws and international standards.

🌡️
Heat Stress Compliance
WBGT is logged every 30 seconds per worker, per shift — creating tamper-proof audit records. When heat zones breach 32°C, automatic break scheduling activates without requiring supervisor intervention.
Factories Act 1948 · Building & Other Construction Workers Act 1996
☀️
UV Radiation Protection
WHO limits daily UV exposure for outdoor workers. Karuna's VEML6075 sensor tracks cumulative UV-A and UV-B dose throughout the day — alerting workers to move to shade before irreversible skin and eye damage occurs.
WHO UV Radiation Guidelines for Outdoor Workers
🚨
Emergency Response SLA
Indian labour law requires emergency response within minutes on hazardous sites. Karuna's fall detection fires G-force + position checks — SOS reaches supervisor with live GPS coordinates in under 3 seconds via any SIM card, no WiFi needed.
OSHA General Duty Clause · Labour Ministry Emergency Guidelines
🛡️
Employer Liability Reduction
Digital safety logs stored per worker per day are ready for legal inspection at any time. Automatic documentation of every alert, rest period, and incident dramatically reduces employer liability in accident claims and insurance disputes.
Employees' Compensation Act 1923 · ESI Act compliance
